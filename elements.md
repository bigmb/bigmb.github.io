<p>Welcome to my home page.
<br>
<br>
I am a Erasmus Mundus Master student in Computer Vision and Robotics (<a href="http://www.vibot.org" target="_blank" >VIBOT</a>) student currently working on my final thesis at <a href="http://www.csiro.au" target="_blank" >CSIRO</a>, Brisbane, Australia. VIBOT is a collaboration between the three leading European institutions, the <a href="http://http://en.u-bourgogne.fr/" target="_blank" >Université de Bourgogne</a> (France), the  <a href="http://www.udg.edu/en/" target="_blank" >Universitat de Girona</a> (Spain),  and <a href="https://www.hw.ac.uk/" target="_blank" >Heriot-Watt University</a> (Scotland).
<br>
	<br>
I obtained my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Pandit Deendayal Petroluem University ( <a href="https://www.pdpu.ac.in/" target="_blank" >PDPU</a>). After the successful completion of Bachelors, I joined <a href="https://www.kuka.com/en-in" target="_blank" >KUKA</a> Robotics India as a Customer Service Engineer and Site Engineer. I have developed most of my research and engineering skills through academic projects, internship, and various competitions. After KUKA Robotics, I joined Keepsake Automation, which is a part of Keepsake Engineering Pvt. Ltd. as an Application Engineer. Here I was in charge of the complete technical aspect of Robotics project. The main challenge was to create a turnkey solution for different customers and design machines according to their requirement.
<br>
	<br>
My research interests are Deep Learning for Medical Imaging and Visual Recognition, Robotics Vision and SLAM , Industrial Robotics.
<br>
	<br>
I am very much interested in a job, with primary focus on Deep Learning applications to Vision and Robotics as well as a fair amount of programming, in academia or in industry. I will be graduating by August-September, 2019, hence, looking for suitable positions worldwide.</p>
